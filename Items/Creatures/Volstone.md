A **volstone** (Kagalarian: *волнок*) is an elemental golem created via magic.

## Appearance
Volstones are floating rocks with a red glowing eye in the middle of them. They weigh around 300 to 400 kilograms and have a diameter of about 66 centimetres.

## Abilities
A volstone flies at a speed of 4 m/s. Using their eye, they can fire a bolt of red lightning at a minimum interval of 5 seconds. Each bolt of lightning has the same destruction capacity as a medium firecracker. It flies at a speed of 4 meters per second.

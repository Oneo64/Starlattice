Niyvod Folensen

Male
Age: 21
Alignment: Neutral Good
Ethnicity: Kagalarian
Race:      Human



[[DESCRIPTION]]
Niyvod is a man from Doryamut, with medium-length brown hair, dark green eyes, and fair skin.

Niyvod wears a dark blue trench coat, dark grey pants, and a pair of black boots. His eyes are downturned, and his eyebrows are thick.

Niyvod works as a programmer in the Kagalarian Military, who is currently working on the battle automation Kag Yakader.



[[PERSONALITY]]
Niyvod is described as a creative person when it comes to solutions. He is also good-hearted but struggles with actually speaking with people, especially with boisterous ones.



[[ABILITIES]]
Niyvod knows many Kagalarian programming languages.

His native language is Kagalarian, and he knows how to speak English.

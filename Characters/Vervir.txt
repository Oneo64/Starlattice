Vervir Dralor

Male
Age: 53
Alignment: Lawful Neutral
Ethnicity: Kagalarian
Race:      Human



[[DESCRIPTION]]
Vervir is a man in the Kagalarian Secret Intelligence Agency. He has green eyes, light blond hair, and fair skin.

He usually wears the KSIA uniform.



[[PERSONALITY]]
Vervir is described as a loyal and wise man. However he has a distrusting nature of other people, and sometimes he can get cocky.



[[ABILITIES]]
Vervir is an expert at Kagalarian martial arts (Dagsar), and he also speaks Kagalarian as his native language.

He also has some degree of stealth and vehicle hijacking skills.

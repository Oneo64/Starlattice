Name: Kagalarian Empire
Creation date: Around the year 800
Main base: Kagalar

Main base description: The Kagalarian Main base is built on a hill, surrounded by snowy coniferous forests. The entrance built on it's side, and it reaches down into the earth. There are 6 floors in the main Kagalarian base, each floor being about 10 meters in height, and 400 meters in width and length. On the hill, there are 4 APMT systems, and 8 anti-spacefighter repeaters. The entrance has a large 10x30 meter gate, and to it's right there is a smaller door. There is a runway connecting the gate to a nearby air and spacecraft field.

Main base floors:
	Ground level: Vehicle and spacecraft building

Fashion:
	Most clothing are simple longsleeved shirt and pants, with no patterns whatsoever. Sometimes someone might wear a trench coat.
	Simple boots are the most common type of footwear.

	Rich men will often wear white metal circlets

Main currency:
	Forgs (Abbrevation: KAGFG)

Political Parties:
	Ovgot: Main political party of the Kagalarian Empire

Military Ranks:
	High Commander (basically a leader, or emperor)
	Commander
	Leader
	Infantry

Uniforms:
	Infantry: White combat helmet, white coat, black utility belt, black boots
	Infantry equipment: KAG Energy Rifle 114, battery recharger, basic radio, three explosive mrokek grenades
	
	High Rank: Any formal clothing, black belt, and a blue surcoat (with a nameplate placed exactly in the middle around the upper chest).

Weapons:
	Standard Weapon: KAG Energy Rifle 114

Troop amount: 10 billion (about a billion per planet)
Spaceship Fleet:
	130 Battleships
	1000 Carriers
	4000 Destroyers
	300,000 Spacefighters

Notes:
	The Kagalarian Army does not use body armor often, because energy bolts are slow, are not as lethal, and can be easily seen.
	Military Personnel are trained to shoot pistols with one hand, as body armor is rarely used.

Name: Kagalarian Empire
Creation date: Around the year 800
Main base: Kagalar

Fashion:
	Most clothing are simple longsleeved shirt and pants, with no patters whatsoever.
	Simple boots are the most common type of footwear.

	Rich men will often wear white metal circlets

Main currency:
	Forgs (Abbrevation: KAGFG)

Political Parties:
	Ovgot: Main political party of the Kagalarian Empire

Military Ranks:
	High Commander (basically a leader, or emperor)
	Commander
	Leader
	Infantry

Uniforms:
	Infantry: White uniform, white combat helmet, gray utility belt
	High Ranks: Any formal clothing, and a blue surcoat. and There is also a nameplate, which is placed exactly in the middle, around the upper chest. A black belt is also worn around the blue vest.

	Most military personnel will have a holster on their belt with an energy pistol

Weapons:
	Standard Weapon: KAG Energy Rifle 114

Troop amount: 32 billion
Spaceship Fleet:
	130 Battleships
	2000 Carriers
	4000 Destroyers
	100000 Spacefighters

Notes:
	The Kagalarian Army does not use body armor often, because energy bolts are slow, are not as lethal, and can be easily seen.
	Military Personnel are trained to shoot pistols with one hand, as body armor is rarely used.

# Vehicles
Vehicles, a long cherished stable of transit, are ground transportation that quickly take people to wherever they need to go. Some vehicles (like civilian vehicles) are optimised for travel and comfort, while others (like military vehicles) are optimised for protection in combat.

## List of Vehicles
### Gaerst Vakaj M-132
 - **Manufacturer:** Gaerst Vehicle Construction Company
 - **Type:** Armored car
 - **Size:** 12 x 4 meters
 - **Maximum Speed:** 25 m/s
 - **Cruising Speed:** 20 m/s
 - **Method of Transportation:** 8 wheels
 - **Main Arnament:** 1 cannon
 - **Secondary Arnament:** 1 high-speed repeater
 - **Crew:** 1 commander, 1 cannon operator, 1 cannon operator's assistant, 1 driver, and 4 spotters

### Gaerst Akraeriy Raekar M-132
 - **Manufacturer:** Gaerst Vehicle Construction Company
 - **Type:** Armored transportation car
 - **Size:** 8 x 3 meters
 - **Maximum Speed:** 35 m/s
 - **Cruising Speed:** 20 m/s
 - **Method of Transportation:** 6 wheels
 - **Arnament:** 1 high-speed repeater
 - **Crew:** 1 driver and 1 high-speed repeater operator
 - **Troop Capacity:** 40 soldiers

### Military Cargo Transportation Truck
 - **Type:** Truck
 - **Size:** 18 x 4 meters
 - **Maximum Speed:** 35 m/s
 - **Cruising Speed:** 20 m/s
 - **Method of Transportation:** 6 wheels
 - **Cargo Capacity:** 65 metric tons

Kagalarian military cargo trucks are built with fresarmanite. Its cargo bed is insulated so that outside heat and electricity won't set off carrying weaponry and explosives.

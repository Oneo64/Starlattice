# Robots
Robots are independent moving machines that are designed for one or two specific tasks, often remotely controlled or autonomous.

## List of Robots
### KAG Yeltol Khamojan Avtit Durst Rovegik (Kag Yakader)
 - **Type:** Super battle robot
 - **Arnament:** 4 High-Speed Repeaters

The Kag Yakader is a robot. It has a rectangular base with 3 wheels on each side, like an armoured car. There is a capsule in the middle of the base, with four guns, one gun built onto each side. The KagYkadr takes about 10 seconds to load up before it can run. There is also a small screen on the front of the robot, which is a debug log that shows the robot's state in loading, or whether the robot sees an enemy, whether it is damaged, etc. The robot can go at a maximum speed of 12 m/s.

KAG Yeltol Khamojan Avtit Durst Rovegik (Kag Yakader)
	Type: Super battle robot
	Weapons: 4 High-Speed Repeaters

	The Kag Yakader is a robot. It has a rectangular base with 3 wheels on each side like an armored car. There is a capsule in the middle of the base, with four guns, a gun built onto each side. The KagYkadr takes about 10 seconds to load up before it can run. There is also a small screen on the front of the robot, which is a debug log that shows the robot's state in loading, or whether the robot sees an enemy, whether it is damaged, etc. The robot can go at a maximum speed of 12 m/s.

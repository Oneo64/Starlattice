# Timeline
## 853
The Seenian Empire is created.

## 858
Ramalosedotan Peace Group is created.

## 1246
Ramalosedotan Paece Group becomes Kagalarian Empire.

## 1530
Mrokek is discovered.

## 1913
Plasma weapons have been invented.

## 1954
Plasma weapons are upgraded to current models, and spacecraft are invented.

## 1960
The first battleship starts construction.

## 1970
The first battleship is constructed.

## 1993
The first space battle happens.

## 2000
The first power armour is invented.

## 2009
Power armours are first used in battle.

## 2010
The Kingdom of Ramalosedot is conquered by the Seenian Empire.

## 2032
The Battle of Samandol on the planet Ramalosedot is fought, an attempt by Kagalar and Escor to regain control of the planet. The invasion failed.

## 2035
The Kagalarian Empire begins to invest in the research of clone technology.

## 2038
The Kagalarian Empire begins to invest in the research and construction of the first battle robot. During the same year, Kagalar initiates the Battle of Sarvikal, fought on one of the Escorian Empire's planets in defence of a Naulish Invasion.

## 3039
The Invasion of Ashunzaria begins.

## 2040
Present date.

## Facts
Battleships have been in construction for 80 years.

[[DESCRIPTION]]
Horinsoler is a large commercial and military city on the south of the continent Kregsorin, about 50,000 hectares in size. Tall buildings and skyscrapers dominate the city.

Military warships can be seen flying overhead patrolling the city, while some commercial spacecraft can be seen flying overhead while they play pre-recorded advertising voicelines.

Roads for cars are on the ground, while there are trains that transport people over the road.



[[BATTLESHIP CONSTRUCTION]]
A battleship construction space station can be seen hovering thousands of kilometers above the city, with about three battleships parked at the station, and one in construction.

Kagalar

Solar System: KAA (KAgalarian-A)



[[CONTINENTS]]
Kregsorin:
	Kregsorin is the largest contenent, which is in the northern hemisphere. It is the only continent on Kagalar with more than one major city.

Arokor:
	Arokor is the second continent of Kagalar, located in the southern hemisphere and on the left side of the planet map. It's a relatively new continent for the Kagalarian Empire, so only a small handful of cities exist on this continent.

Akrin:
	Akrin is the island south of Arokor, a land of complete wilderness. It is already throughoutly explored, and now acts as a land for bombardment and weapons testing.

Febelen:
	Febelen is the the third continent, which is in the southern hemisphere and on the right of the planet map. This continent primarily acts as a manufacturing and resource gathering land.

Eluniel:
	Eluniel is the island located southwest of Febelen.
